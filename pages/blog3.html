003- Elements of Composition Photo

3/2/2023

My favourite photo I took for the 'Elements of Photography Scavenger Hunt' is the photo for Contrast through Colour.

I chose this photo for 'Contrast through colour' because of the start contrast of colour between the yellow road blockers/fire hydrant and the white, colourless background of everything else around it. It contrast it made in the photo even makes it look a bit unnatural, despite not being edited with Photoshop.

When I took the photos for the 'Elements of Photography Scavenger Hunt' I learned that almost everything can be photographed into something interesting, with enough creativity and opportunity. I learned this as I was taking seemingly random and meaningless photos, that with enough thought and meaning put into it, any photo of anything can turn into something artistic and interesting.

When taking this photo, I also learned that colour in photo can be very unique and different across different photos, even without the presence of colour. Colour in photos can be used to mix multiple things together, create contrast between objects, create moods or feelings in the viewer, and even more. Even colour-less photos like black-and-white can create contrast and thoughts without colour.

Also, for anyone curious, it's spelt colour, not color.
